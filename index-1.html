Best Seller: Katra to Katra

Our most popular 6 Nights / 7 Days package starting and ending at Katra Railway Station.

Day 01: Arrival & Drive to Pahalgam

Pickup from Katra. Drive via Chenani-Nashri Tunnel (India's Longest). Check-in at Hotel Kloof Orchid (Centrally Heated).

Day 02: Pahalgam Sightseeing

Visit Aru, Betaab Valley & Chandanwari. Evening drive to Gulmarg. Overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day 03: Gulmarg to Srinagar

Gondola Ride (Phase 1 & 2). Visit Golf Course. Drive to Srinagar. Stay at Hotel Chinar Inn.

Day 04: Sonmarg Excursion

Day trip to Sonmarg (Meadow of Gold). Visit Thajiwas Glacier. Night stay at Nazneen Houseboats (Dal Lake).

Day 05: Doodhpathri Trip

Explore the lush meadows of Doodhpathri. Return to Srinagar for shopping and leisure.

Day 06: Srinagar Leisure

Shankaracharya Temple, Mughal Gardens. 1 Hour Complimentary Shikara Ride. Overnight Srinagar.

Day 07: Departure

Drop at Katra Railway Station with sweet memories.
